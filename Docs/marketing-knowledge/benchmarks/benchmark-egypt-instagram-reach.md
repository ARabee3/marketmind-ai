## The exact figure(s)

Instagram had **23,949,400 users in Egypt in June 2026**, equal to **20.1%**
of the population. The largest age group was **18–24** with **8.7 million**
users. **52.7%** of users were men.

## Methodology (who measured it, how, when)

Figures are published by NapoleonCat from their "Social Media Users in
Egypt" dashboard, June 2026 snapshot. The numbers are platform-reported
advertising audience estimates (the audience reachable through Meta Ads),
aggregated by age and gender, returned for the country of Egypt.

## What it should and should not be used to claim

**May be used to claim:** the size and demographic shape of the Instagram
advertising audience in Egypt as of June 2026 — i.e. that Instagram reaches a
meaningful, younger-adult-skewing audience in Egypt, smaller than
Facebook's, with the 18–24 band as the largest cohort. This supports
choosing Instagram for objectives targeting a younger adult Egyptian
audience and for visual businesses.

**Should not be used to claim:** unique human individuals (platform ad
audiences overcount); specific sub-city reach; engagement rate, CPC, CPM,
or conversion likelihood — see `engagement-rate-benchmark-caveat.md`. It
should not be used to argue Instagram is bigger than Facebook in Egypt
(the figure is materially lower).

## Expiry rationale

Same as the Facebook reach entry: platform-reach figures move quarterly, so
a six-month freshness window (effective June 2026, expires December 2026)
is appropriate. After expiry the entry must not be used for live retrieval
