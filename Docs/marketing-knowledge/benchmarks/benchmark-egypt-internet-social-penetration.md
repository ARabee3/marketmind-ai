## The exact figure(s)

At the end of 2025, Egypt had **98.2 million internet users** (**82.7%**
online penetration). In October 2025, Egypt had **51.6 million social media
user identities** (**43.4%** of the population). In late 2025, **121
million cellular mobile connections** were active (**102%** of population —
this reflects multi-SIM ownership and is not a unique-subscriber count).

## Methodology (who measured it, how, when)

Figures are published by DataReportal as part of "Digital 2026: Egypt,"
drawn from a range of official and platform-reported sources, aggregated to
country level. Internet and social figures are point-in-time snapshots (end
of 2025 for internet; October 2025 for social identities); the mobile
figure is a late-2025 connections count.

## What it should and should not be used to claim

**May be used to claim:** that Egypt has high internet penetration (a
large online population) and a substantial social-media user base, which
justifies digital-first channels as the default for Egyptian SME
marketing. The mobile figure supports mobile-first design of any website
or landing page.

**Should not be used to claim:** unique social media users — note the entry
says "user identities" by design, because one person often holds multiple
accounts; this overcounts humans. The **121 million mobile connections**
must never be reported as "121 million unique mobile subscribers" — it is
102% of population precisely because of multi-SIM ownership, and saying
otherwise would be a methodology error. This figure is also country-level
and says nothing about sub-market (urban/rural, governorate) penetration.
It should not be used to claim engagement, conversion, or per-channel
reach (use the channel-specific reach entries for those).

## Expiry rationale

These are annual macro figures; a one-year freshness window (effective
2026-01-01, expires 2026-12-31) is appropriate. DataReportal publishes a new
Egypt report annually; after expiry the entry is unavailable for live
retrieval until a refreshed entry is authored.
