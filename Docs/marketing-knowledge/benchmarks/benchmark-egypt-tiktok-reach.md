## The exact figure(s)

DataReportal's "Digital 2026: Egypt" reports that TikTok's advertising
resources showed **48.8 million users aged 18+** in Egypt in late 2025.
The same report states that TikTok ads reached **65.5% adult ad reach** for
people aged 18 and above in Egypt at the end of 2025.

## Methodology (who measured it, how, when)

DataReportal republishes figures from TikTok advertising resources. These are
ad-planning audience figures, not monthly active-user figures and not unique
people counts. TikTok's tools can target ads to users aged 13 and above, but
the published planning data is limited to users aged 18+.

TikTok's official Reach Estimator documentation says reach forecasts depend on
campaign settings such as budget, targeting, optimization, and bidding
strategy. TikTok also says forecast accuracy can vary over time and gives no
guarantee of a specific audience size, result, or outcome. Egypt is listed in
the Reach Estimator availability regions.

## What it should and should not be used to claim

**May be used to claim:** TikTok has a large addressable adult ad audience in
Egypt, so it can be considered for awareness and acquisition tests when the
business has suitable video capacity and a clear conversion offramp.

**Should not be used to claim:** monthly active users, unique people, guaranteed
campaign reach, engagement rate, conversion likelihood, cost per outcome, or
Egypt-specific CPC / CPM. It is a reach-planning benchmark only, not CPC, not
CPM, and not a performance guarantee.
