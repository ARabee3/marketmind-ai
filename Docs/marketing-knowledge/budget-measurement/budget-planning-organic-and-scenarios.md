## When useful

Use this playbook whenever the plan involves any paid spend at all — which
budget mode applies, how much to commit, and how to size it to the owner's
real capacity rather than an aspiration. Also useful for choosing
`organic_only` deliberately when there is genuinely no money to spend.

## Poor-fit conditions

A poor fit when the owner cannot say what they can spend per month without
interrupting it — a budget has to be sustainable across the 12-week
window or the plan's results become unreadable. Also a poor fit when the
owner wants to set a budget from an assumed CPC/CPM they read online (none
are verified for Egypt — see `engagement-rate-benchmark-caveat.md`);
budget must start from the owner's real cash, not from an assumed rate.

## Required inputs

- The chosen budget mode (`organic_only`, `monthly_amount`,
  `three_month_amount`, `scenario_only`).
- Owner's sustainable monthly cash for marketing, in EGP.
- The objective and the KPI the budget is meant to move.
- A small "test-first" portion to learn before committing the rest.

## Worked micro-example (illustrative — not real EGP figures)

> The numbers below are illustrative placeholders chosen to show the
> shape of calculation, not real market rates or verified benchmarks.

Suppose an owner can sustain **3,000 EGP/month** and the objective is
acquisition over 12 weeks.

- **Mode:** `monthly_amount` — commit 3,000 EGP/month for the 12 weeks =
  roughly 9,000 EGP total, but released flat (3,000/month) so a stop or
  resume mid-plan does not break it.
- **Test-first split:** run the first **1,000 EGP** as a 5–7 day test to
  learn a rough cost-per-new-customer, then either continue or adjust.
- **Measurement anchor:** count *new customers* each week and the spend
  that produced them, not clicks alone. If the test shows cost per new
  customer is clearly unprofitable after margin, stop and re-plan before
  spending the remaining 8,000.

For `scenario_only` (e.g. a launch): set a one-window budget for the
launch weeks only (illustratively, 5,000 EGP concentrated in launch week +
week-after follow-through), then revert to organic. For `three_month_amount`
the discipline is the same but the commitment is to a quarter, with a
test-first check before the second month is locked. For `organic_only`
there is no cash spend; the budget is the time cost of the owners and
helpers, which still must be honest and reserved.
