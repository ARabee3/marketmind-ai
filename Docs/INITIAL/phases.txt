MarketMind AI is not just a tool; it's an Autonomous AI Marketing Manager that handles the entire marketing cycle for SMEs in 5 simple steps:

1. The Discovery (The Interview)
Instead of complex forms, the user has a "chat" with our Discovery Agent. This AI asks smart questions about their business, goals, budget, and competitors.  
• Purpose: To build a deep understanding of the business (The "Business Profile").  

2. The Strategy (The Roadmap)
Our Strategy Agent analyzes (Search Engine) the interview data and creates a custom 3-month (according the plan) marketing plan. It decides which platforms to use (TikTok, Instagram, etc.), how to spend the budget, and what KPIs to track.  

3. The Content (The Engine)
The Content Agent automatically generates everything needed for the strategy: Arabic/English captions, AI-generated images, and video scripts for Reels/TikTok—all matching the brand's unique voice.  

4. The Execution (The Publisher)
Using n8n automation, the system automatically schedules and posts the content to social media platforms. The business owner doesn’t need to touch a single button.  

5. The Optimization (The Pivot)
The Monitor Agent tracks performance 24/7. If engagement drops or an ad is underperforming, the Pivot Engine sends an instant alert to the owner with a specific suggestion on what to change to get better results.  

Summary: We are building a "Closed Loop" system: Learn → Plan → Create → Publish → Monitor → Improve. Our goal is to give a small cafe or a local boutique the same marketing power as a big company, at a fraction of the cost.
